AppWorks Launcher Demo

You have two buttons below which interact with the AWLauncher plugin

"Get Opening URL" will retrieve the URL used to open the client

"Clear Opening URL" will clear the URL used to open the client.

You may wish to clear the opening URL so that your app doesn't process it multiple times.

An example URL to open the client may be: x-otm://launchUrl=http%3A%2F%2Fmyapp.mydomain.com%2Fmyservice%2Fdata%2F123

Opening URL will appear here
